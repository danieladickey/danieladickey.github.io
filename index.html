Who is Daniel A Dickey?

In a wonderful partnership, Daniel is joined in marriage to his amazing wife, Nicole. Together, they share a passion for musicals, kayaking adventures, and savoring culinary experiences. Hailing from Utah, Daniel's early days were marked by a fondness for Legos, technology, and the great outdoors. Following high school, his deep-rooted patriotism and strong yearning to serve motivated him to enlist in the US Army, where he dedicated four years of service, including three combat deployments to Iraq.

Fuelled by his love for running and unyielding determination, Daniel achieved an impressive feat that most don't even consider attempting, running a marathon. Completing the Charlotte Marathon on two separate occasions, conquering the demanding 26.2-mile required consistency, self-awareness, discipline, time, and heart.

Even amidst a global pandemic, Daniel's unwavering determination propelled him to achieve a significant milestone: he earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Utah State University, graduating in 2021. Following his academic success, Daniel embarked on his professional journey as a software engineer at a multinational IT consulting firm. There, he harnesses his profound affinity for technology and natural curiosity to contribute meaningfully to his role.
