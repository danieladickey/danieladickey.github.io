What is agile?

Agile is an idea or philosophy. In software development, agile practices involve discovering requirements and developing solutions through the collaborative effort of self-organizing and cross-functional teams and their customer/end user.

What the heck is Scrum?

Scrum is the most popular agile framework, with 66 percent of all agile frameworks being scrum or scrum variants. Scrum is an agile framework for developing, delivering, and sustaining complex products, with an initial emphasis on software development, although it has been used in other fields including research, sales, marketing and advanced technologies.
